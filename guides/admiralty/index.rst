Admiralty
#########

Admiralty is our internal dashboard and management tool which allows ItsMyCargo
employees to have overall visibility on the shops as well manage every aspect of the
shops that has no other means of managing (i.e. in Dipper or Bridge).

Access
======

To have access to Admiralty, user must have ItsMyCargo Google Workspace account
(`@itsmycargo.com` email address).

To access Admiralty, visit `https://api.itsmycargo.com/admin <https://api.itsmycargo.com/admin>`_
